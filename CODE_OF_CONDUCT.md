# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to foster an open, welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in the BizInsight AI community.

BizInsight AI is an AI-powered platform that transforms customer reviews into actionable business insights through sentiment analysis, issue detection, trend tracking, and intelligent recommendations. We welcome contributors from all backgrounds who are passionate about AI, data analytics, and open-source collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive community include:

- Treating everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Providing constructive and respectful feedback.
- Collaborating openly and sharing knowledge.
- Supporting contributors of all experience levels.
- Respecting different viewpoints and technical approaches.
- Writing clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.
- Supporting the project and, if you find it valuable, considering giving the repository a ⭐ star.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Publishing confidential or sensitive information without permission.
- Deliberately introducing malicious, misleading, or insecure code.
- Any behavior that creates a hostile or unwelcoming environment.

## Responsible Development

As BizInsight AI processes customer feedback and AI-generated insights, contributors are expected to:

- Follow secure coding and data privacy practices.
- Protect confidential and sensitive information.
- Build transparent, reliable, and maintainable AI features.
- Test changes thoroughly before submitting a Pull Request.
- Document significant changes and improvements clearly.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards and may take appropriate action when necessary.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and official community communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers through the project's official communication channels.
